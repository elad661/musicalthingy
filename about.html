Elad's Musical Thingy

This is a thing (a game?) that I made. It plays music when you move the mouse.

How to Play

I think it's more fun if you figure it out yourself, but if you really want to know, move the mouse up and down to control which note will be played, and move the mouse left and right to control the panning. It's really simple.
